Keilly Guevara Flores

DIGITAL PORTFOLIO

Hello there! Welcome to my home page! I am Keilly Guevara, a senior at UNLV majoring in Graphic Design. I was born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada. My family is from Guatemala. I love to spend time with my family watching movies, as well as baking, reading, drawing, and painting. Lately I have delved into reading classical books, which inspired me to make some illustrations on the Count of Monte Cristo for one of my class assignments. On the other pages you will find my class projects from illustration, graphics, photography, and sculpture. Feel free to take a look around and explore.
