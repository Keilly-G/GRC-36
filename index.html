Hello there! Welcome to my home page! I am Keilly Guevara, a senior at UNLV majoring in Graphic Design. Some background information about myself, my family is from Guatemala but I was born and raised here in Las Vegas. In my free time I love to spend time with my family watching movies, as well as baking, reading, drawing, and painting. Lately I have delved into reading classical books, I was able to make some illustrations on the Count of Monte Cristo for one of my class assignments.

On this website you will find my class projects from illustration, graphics, photography, and sculpture.
